Luvlin Mental Health Community mental health tips

A list of mental health tips to help improve your mental health.

Note: The tips are not guaranteed to work.

Journaling

Track your gratitude, achievements, and feelings with a journal every day. You can reflect on this later and see eventual improvements.

Positivity

Try to think positively. Instead of ''I can't do it'', you should think ''I might be able to do at least a part of this! Let's try it!'' for example.

Creativity

Be creative. Creative expression and your mental health are linked with each other. You can make some art, write a poem, etc.

Love

Express to the people who you love that you love them.

Laughing

Laugh as much as you can. It has a positive effect on your mental and physical health.

Distraction

Find distraction in tough times. Hang out with friends, play games, go outside, and do other things you might enjoy.

Warm bath

Take a relaxing warm bath every week.

Pets

Try to spend some time with your pets. They have a positive impact on your mental health.

Venting

Expressing your emotions is good when you feel bad. You can write it down on paper or use the venting channels on this server.

Forgiving

Attempt to forgive people who have done something wrong to you. Forgiving is not the same as forgetting, but you'll get peace with the situation and no longer feel anger and hate towards the person who has done something wrong to you. Forcing it is not good as that works contradictory.

Smiling

Try to smile when you're feeling stressed.

Gratefulness

Express gratefulness towards the people you are grateful for.

Sunshine

Try getting at least 15 minutes of sunshine daily.

Walking

Try to walk for at least 30 minutes each day, preferably outside.

Friends & Family

Spend time with your friends and family.

Skills

Learn new skills.

Value yourself

Value yourself, avoid criticizing yourself or being disrespectful to yourself

Boundaries

Mention your boundaries when people are crossing them.

Eating healthy

Eat healthy meals.

Hydration

Drink enough water.

Avoid unhealthy things

Avoid smoking, vaping, and other unhealthy activities.

Enough sleep

It's important to get at least 7 hours of sleep per night.

Realistic

Set realistic goals whenever you want to achieve something. You don't need to put unnecessary stress on yourself.

Avoid negativity

Try to avoid people who talk bad about you or don't believe in you achieving goals.

Don't be harsh about yourself

It's okay when you don't achieve your goals, don't be too harsh about it.
