Luvlin Mental Health Community guidelines

Novi wants everyone to have a warm welcome in this community. We have the server guidelines to keep this community a safe place.

Our community guidelines help you to make clear what is not allowed in the Luvlin Mental Health Community. We are a heavily moderated community.

Everyone who visits and participates in the Luvlin Mental Health Community agrees to follow the guidelines to keep this community a peaceful environment for everyone.

The spirit of the community guidelines is more important than the letter. If we come across content or messages violating the nature of the guidelines, we have the right to take action.

If someone violates the Luvlin Mental Health Community guidelines, we may take moderation actions against them. This includes removing the messages, forcing nickname changes, warning the user, muting the user, (temporarily) banning the user, and reporting to Discord Trust & Safety. When handling reports, the Luvlin Mental Health Community moderation team might consider off-server content (DM's and other Discord servers).

Respect our guidelines and keep the community enjoyable for everyone!

Harassment

Harassment of other members is prohibited. Targeted harassment will result in an immediate permanent ban..

Exposing and slandering

Do not ''expose'' people. Do not slander others. Do not ruin people's reputations without (or with forged) evidence. It will be a reason for a ban.

Discriminatory or derogatory behavior

Sending discriminatory content or messages is not allowed. This includes the usage of derogatory slurs and generalizing groups.

NSFW

Posting or discussing NSFW content/messages is not allowed.

Encouragement of self-destructive behavior

Encouragement of self-destructive behavior results in a permanent ban.

Dating

The Discord server is not a dating server. Do not mention that you are ''single'' and do not use the Discord server to look for romantic contacts.

Private information & doxxing

Do not leak private information about yourself or others. Doxxing results in harsh consequences.

Malicious links

Posting malicious links such as IP-grabbers, token-loggers, and malware will result in a permanent ban.

Swearing

Please do not swear excessively and refrain from swearing targeted at others.

Auto-moderation

Do not bypass the auto-moderation.

Advertising

Advertising other Discord servers or social media is not allowed without prior approval from the management team.

Spamming

Do not spam the Discord server.

Discord bot abuse

Abusing the Discord server bots is prohibited. This includes but is not limited to, spamming to try to get XP, using the PluralKit bot to roleplay, and scamming.

Censoring of TW-topics

Please censor sensitive subjects.

Boundaries

Please respect people's boundaries. This includes respecting people's pronouns, DM status, and venting status.

Languages

Please only talk in English or Japanese on the Discord server. We prefer it if you keep in-depth Japanese conversations in the channels with ''JP'' before the channel name.

Languages 2

Channels with ''JP'' before the Discord channel name are Japanese-only.

Nicknames

Having a pingable and appropriate (nick)name is mandatory.

Drama and arguments

Do not cause drama and arguments in the Discord server chat.

Encouraging rule-breaking

Encouragement or facilitating rule-breaking is disallowed.

Evasion of punishments

Evading any form of Discord server-level issued punishments will result in a permanent ban on all associated accounts.

Abuse of our support service

Do not abuse our support service by making false reports, using forged evidence, making troll tickets, and more.

Copying

Copying the server or using heavy inspiration is not allowed.

Inactivity in chat

Please do not say ''dead chat'' or similar phrases.

Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines

Everyone is required to follow the Discord Terms of Service & Community Guidelines.
