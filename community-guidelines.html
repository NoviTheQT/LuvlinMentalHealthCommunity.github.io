Luvlin Mental Health Community Discord Server Guidelines

Welcome to the Luvlin Mental Health Community Discord server. These guidelines are in place to create a safe and supportive environment for all members. We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a positive atmosphere within our community.

Our community guidelines are essential to fostering a respectful and secure environment within the Luvlin Mental Health Community. We are dedicated to maintaining a well-moderated platform that promotes a sense of safety and inclusivity. By participating in this community, all members agree to adhere to these guidelines to ensure a harmonious experience for everyone.

Please remember that the intent and spirit of these guidelines hold greater significance than their exact wording. In cases where content or messages are found to violate the underlying principles of these guidelines, appropriate action may be taken.

If any member breaches the Luvlin Mental Health Community guidelines, the moderation team reserves the right to take appropriate measures. These measures may include message removal, enforcing nickname changes, issuing warnings, muting users, imposing (temporary) bans, and reporting violations to Discord's Trust & Safety team. It is important to note that when handling reports, the Luvlin Mental Health Community moderation team may consider off-server content, such as direct messages and interactions in other Discord servers.

Harassment

Harassment of any community member is strictly prohibited. Targeted harassment will result in an immediate permanent ban.

Exposing and slandering

Do not expose individuals or engage in slanderous behavior. Avoid damaging someone's reputation without proper evidence, whether genuine or fabricated. Violations of this nature will lead to disciplinary action.

Discriminatory or derogatory behavior

Sharing discriminatory content or messages is not permitted. This includes the use of derogatory slurs or making generalizations about specific groups.

NSFW content

Posting or discussing Not Safe for Work (NSFW) content or messages is prohibited.

Encouragement of self-destructive behavior

Any form of encouragement or promotion of self-destructive behavior will result in a permanent ban.

Dating

This server is not a dating platform. Mentioning your relationship status, sexual interest, or seeking romantic connections is not allowed.

Private information & doxxing

Sharing private information about yourself or others is not allowed. Engaging in doxxing will result in severe consequences.

Malicious links

Posting malicious links such as IP-grabbers, token-loggers, and malware will result in a permanent ban.

Language and behavior

While some swearing may be tolerated, excessive and targeted profanity is prohibited.

Auto-moderation

Bypassing the auto-moderation system is prohibited.

Advertisements and promotions

Advertising other Discord servers or social media platforms requires prior approval from the management team.

Spam

Avoid spamming the Discord server with excessive or repetitive messages.

Discord bot abuse

Abusing server bots, including spamming for XP, roleplaying with the PluralKit bot, or engaging in scams, is not allowed.

Sensitive and triggering subjects

When discussing sensitive or triggering subjects, please use appropriate content warnings (TW) and spoilers as needed.

Boundaries

Always respect others' boundaries, including pronouns, direct messaging preferences, and venting status.

Foreign languages

Communicate primarily in English or Japanese on this server. In-depth Japanese conversations are encouraged in channels labeled with "JP" before the channel name.

Designated language channels

Channels labeled with "JP" are exclusively for Japanese language conversations.

Nicknames

Maintain a pingable and appropriate nickname.

Drama and arguments

Refrain from engaging in drama and arguments within the server chat.

Encouragement of violations

Encouraging or facilitating violations of the server guidelines is not allowed.

Punishment evasion

Evading server-level punishments will result in a permanent ban across all associated accounts.

Responsible usage of support services

Avoid misuse of our support services, such as false reports, forged evidence, and troll tickets.

Originality and copying

Do not replicate the server or heavily draw inspiration from it.

Chat activity

Avoid making statements like "dead chat" or similar expressions.

Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines

All members are required to adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
